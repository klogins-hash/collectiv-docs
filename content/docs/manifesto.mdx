## What We're Building

The Collectiv is a creator cooperative. Member-owned. Member-operated. Built for creators who want to own what they make and build wealth together.

**Not a platform.** A project.

**Not finished.** Just started.

**Not selling you something.** Inviting you to build something.

### The Structure

We're legally structured as a [Public Benefit Corporation (PBC)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Public-benefit_corporation) organized as a cooperative.

**What this means:**
- Members own the cooperative
- Members govern the cooperative (one member, one vote)
- Mission is legally protected
- Value flows to those who create it

**The three vows:**
1. We will never take traditional Venture Capital
2. We will never go IPO
3. We will never sell to another company

**We're member-owned, forever.**

## The Economic Model

**Traditional platforms:** You pay fees forever. They own the infrastructure. You're a renter.

**Traditional cooperatives:** Complex governance. Unclear value. Hard to join.

**The Collectiv:** You crowdfund to get in. You own your IP and cooperative equity. You borrow against your equity to build wealth.

### How It Works

**Step 1: Crowdfund to Join**

Your contribution goes into the general cooperative pool. Think of it as buying equity in a startup, except it's a cooperative and you actually own part of it.

**Step 2: Own Your IP + Equity**

You own everything you create (100%). You also own cooperative equity in everything the pool creates. Your equity grows as the cooperative grows.

**Step 3: Build Wealth**

Don't cash out. Borrow against your equity instead (like billionaires do). No taxes. Your equity keeps growing. We handle all the boring financial stuff.

**This is how you build real wealth.**
