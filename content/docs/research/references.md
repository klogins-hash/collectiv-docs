## Historical Examples

### Successful Cooperatives

**[Mondragon Corporation](https://www.mondragon-corporation.com/en/)**
- Founded 1956 in Basque Country, Spain
- 80+ cooperatives, 80,000+ worker-owners
- â‚¬12 billion annual revenue
- 70 years of sustained success

**[Emilia-Romagna Cooperatives](https://www.legacoop.coop/)**
- Region in Italy with strong cooperative tradition
- 30%+ of GDP from cooperatives
- Demonstrates regional cooperative economics

**[Rochdale Pioneers](https://www.rochdalepioneersmuseum.coop/)**
- Founded 1844 in England
- Established cooperative principles still used today
- Historical foundation of cooperative movement

### Failed or Challenged Cooperatives

- Israeli kibbutzim (many privatized)
- Soviet collective farms (forced, failed)
- 1960s-70s US cooperatives (most didn't scale)

**Lessons:** Scale, sustainability, and cultural fit matter
